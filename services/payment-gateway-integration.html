Our process

How we integrate your payment gateway

A focused, technical process — from understanding your payment flow to integrating the API, setting up webhooks and ensuring every transaction is tracked correctly.

01
Review requirements & payment flow

We identify how your checkout works, what payment methods you need (FPX, card, e-wallet) and how successful payments should update your orders or system records.

02
API integration & webhook setup

We connect your system to the selected payment gateway, implement API requests, configure secure webhooks and handle payment statuses like success, failed, pending or expired.

03
Testing, verification & go-live

We test real transactions, edge cases and callback responses, ensure accurate syncing to your system, then deploy it live with monitoring and post-launch support.
