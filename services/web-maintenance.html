Our process

How we maintain and care for your website

A structured maintenance workflow — keeping your website secure, updated and performing well, while reducing the time you spend on technical issues.

01
Website audit & setup

We review your hosting, CMS, plugins, performance, and security risks, then configure backups, monitoring tools and maintenance access.

02
Security & system updates

We apply system updates, fix vulnerabilities, patch plugins and ensure everything runs on the latest, safest version.

03
Content & minor changes

We update text, images, banners, promotions and make small improvements — keeping your site fresh and aligned with your latest business needs.

04
Monitoring & support

We monitor uptime, speed and errors, restore backups when needed and provide ongoing support for small fixes or adjustments.
