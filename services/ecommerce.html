Our process

How we build your e-commerce system

A clear, step-by-step process from understanding your products and workflows to launching a stable, ready-to-use e-commerce system.

01
Requirements & scoping

We understand your products, pricing, shipping rules, payment methods and internal workflows, then define a clear system scope together.

02
Flows & structure planning

We plan catalog structure, customer journey, order statuses, roles and permissions so the whole e-commerce flow is organised and scalable.

03
Design, development & integrations

We design the storefront and dashboards, build all key modules and connect payment gateways, shipping and any required third-party tools.

04
Testing, launch & support

We test real scenarios (orders, stock, payments), deploy to your hosting and support your team with training, fixes and future improvements.
