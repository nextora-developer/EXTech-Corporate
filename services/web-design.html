Landing page

Landing pages that focus on one clear action

A landing page is designed to do one thing very well — capture leads, drive sign-ups or sell a specific offer with no distractions.

  • One goal, one page

    Perfect for ads, campaigns and promotions with a single, clear call-to-action.

  • Built to convert

    Sections are structured for attention → interest → proof → action.

  • Easy to test & improve

    Headline, layout and CTA can be refined over time to improve results.

Hero Section
  • Clear headline (what you do + value)
  • Short supporting sub-headline
  • Primary CTA (WhatsApp / Contact Form / Book Call)
Key Benefits / Value Proposition
  • 3–5 short benefit points
  • Focus on outcomes, not features
  • Icons or simple visuals
Product / Service Overview
  • Brief explanation of the offer
  • What the service/product includes
  • Who it is for
How It Works
  • Step 1: Contact / Submit
  • Step 2: Process
  • Step 3: Delivery / Result
Testimonials
  • Client logos
  • Reviews or short quotes
Call To Action (CTA)
  • Strong action-oriented message
  • Button linking to WhatsApp / Form
  • Repeated once more near bottom
FAQ
  • 3–6 common questions
  • Pricing, timeline, revisions, support
Footer

  • Social links
  • Copyright
